BOHYEMIAN-LAB
Bohyemian [at BOHYEMIAN.LAB] conducts design research projects focusing on data-driven self-expression through walking, reading, and more. She collects her diverse personal data, and explores related topics through utilizing multiple approaches such as Data Visualization, Computational Art & Design, Visual Communication Design, and Technology.
