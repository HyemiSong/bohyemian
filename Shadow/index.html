Shadow from the Sun
  • Shadow from the Sun is a sculptural data art project that depicts the correlation between the number of sunspots and the climate of the Earth through the causality between light and shadow. This project has the goals of exploring ways of conveying information and aesthetic value through utilizing data as an art medium in physical space.
